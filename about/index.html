About Me

A little bit more about me

I've decided to reinvent myself by learning to code. My journey into tech started by volunteering to assist a close friend with a budding music career. As I began to gradually create content to enhance this musician's online presence, I was amazed at how quickly technology can change
the trajectory of one's goals.

As time progressed, I found myself enjoying the ability to
create so decided to learn how to build my own website. I'm hoping to eventually make a complete migration to front-end development,
where I can use my persistence, creativity, and organization to write clean, elegant code.
